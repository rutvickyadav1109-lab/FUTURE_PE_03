Why This Internal Linking Strategy Helps SEO
This structure follows the pillar-and-cluster model used in modern SEO. The pillar blog acts as the main authority page, while each supporting article covers a more specific topic and links back to the main guide.
These internal links help search engines understand how the content is related, strengthening the website's topical authority around men's and boys' fashion in Boisar. They also encourage visitors to explore additional articles, increasing page views, reducing bounce rate, and helping users spend more time on the website.
